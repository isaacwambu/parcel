About Us

Maribel-Hermans Associates is a consultancy and accountancy firm licensed under the Accountants Act number 15 of 2008 offering audit, tax and advisory services to clients in various industries. Our team serves and offer quality services across industries and sectors in the East Africa region. We are dedicated in client service delivery with akin to quality and meeting customer needs and standards.

Our Values

We uphold public trust in providing services. Our core values of excellence, innovation, integrity, leadership, respect, care and teamwork underpins the way we work and provide our clients with quality services.

Our People

Maribel-Hermans pride itself on operational excellence, attributable to our highly competent & diverse workforce. We work hard to ensure we recruit the right person for the right job & through our individual performance management processes evaluate and develop the capability and competence of our people. This includes a successful leadership development & graduate program. We value employees, not just for what they do, but the way they do it & we demonstrate our appreciation through our reward & recognition schemes. Maribel-Hermans’ people are passionate about their work.
